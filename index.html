About us

Who we are

At House of Prayer International Church, we believe that everyone is loved by God and has a place in His kingdom. Located in the heart of Berlin, we are a welcoming, multicultural congregation where people from all backgrounds can come together in faith.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to love God, love people, and make a difference. Through prayer, worship, and acts of service, we seek to bring God’s light into our city and beyond. Whether you're looking for a church home, seeking answers, or just want to experience the power of prayer, we invite you to join us.

Come as you are—let’s grow in faith together!
